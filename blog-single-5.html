AnyX--Accompanying robot

by Ye Kaiyi / Product Design, Design Research

At present, the rapid growth of empty-nest youth in our country, the proportion of one-family family system increases year by year, the number of only child rises, the children's loneliness is increasingly aggravated...... Keeping a pet can satisfy their spiritual needs and bring them a sense of companionship and security. However, not everyone can afford to keep a pet.

AnyX is an intelligent pet robot that can simulate the behavior and emotion of pets. It can provide companion, entertainment and treatment functions, and provide empty-nest youth with company, stress relief, entertainment, health management, social interaction and other benefits, bringing more fun and convenience to their life.
