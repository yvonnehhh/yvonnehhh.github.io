"crawfish Man"-- crawfish shell recycling project

by Ye Kaiyi / Product Design, Service Design, Design Research

From the perspective of environmental protection, the "Crawfish man" project starts from the waste of crawfish shells on the dining table, opens up the market of food waste classification and recycling in the catering industry, and helps socially disadvantaged groups to set up an industrial chain for the reuse of food waste resources.
