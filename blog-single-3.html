"Journey of a Thousand Miles"-- Fitting shoes for mountain children

by Ye Kaiyi / Product design,Service Design

The Daliang Mountains in Sichuan Province, at an altitude of 2,000-3,500 metres, are isolated from the rest of the world. The only road to the outside world is a pitted dirt and stone road. The children here measure the world with their feet every day, but a journey of a thousand miles is not as easy as taking the first step.

Therefore, we decided to start from the demand, designing a pair of shoes that can be adjusted in both directions, can be assembled at any time, are wear-resistant and waterproof, and are warm and practical. At the same time, we will play the roles of the enterprise, society and government to solve the difficulties of poor children's shoes and help them grow up healthily.

"Journey of a Thousand Miles" Science and Technology Poverty Alleviation Project Led the school project team to the top 20 in the country (20/153) and received $2,000 in support funds.
