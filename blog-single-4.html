Intelligent self-regulation portable knee pads

by Ye Kaiyi / Product Design, Interaction Design, Service Design, Design Research

Outdoor sports people's awareness of knee health protection needs to be improved. Functional requirements for functional knee pads are mainly manifested as auxiliary sports training and health monitoring functions on the basis of satisfying basic protective functions. Therefore, in the development of this functional knee protection product, sensors are used to monitor and warn knee health, and a set of knee protection, monitoring and warning system is established to improve its protection quality. It is a comprehensive intelligent wearable knee protection equipment.
